Research

RMC aims to do research on a continuous basis on financial risk management, and management of risk in the financial services industry. Initial research is to establish the current status of risk management by identifying the key risks that the Kenyan Financial Industry faces and the mitigation options.

Training

RMC helps organizations managing risk and/or disaster in curriculum design and development; executive workshops; training of trainers; employee and management training, individual and group coaching, etc.

Consulting

RMC serves as a center for networking for continuous engagement and dissemination of educational and professional development resources for scholars and organizations, as well as to students, interested in risk and disaster management.

About Us

The Risk Management Centre (RMC), established in July 2014, is a Research and Consultancy Centre focusing on risk management and disaster management under a common platform of scholarly research and technical expertise. We are unique in the sense that we are the first of a kind in the region to address risk and disaster management issues under one roof. The RMC focuses on risk management covering various aspects at the micro and institutional levels of market and industry risk, their modelling and management. Disaster management is the second-arm of the centre which involves monitoring and managing natural as well as man-made disasters such as flooding, famine, health epidemics, political violence, etc. These two-arms of research on risk and disaster are founded on and share the common platform in the RMC, which comprises various important databases and a pool of scholarly and industrial experts for the monitoring and analysis of risk and disaster in the East and Central African region.
